Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

What's included in your web design packages?

Our comprehensive packages include custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, free hosting for one year, SSL certificate, and 3 months of post-launch support. We also provide training for your team to manage content updates.

How long does it take to complete a website project?

Typical projects take 4-6 weeks from start to finish. Complex e-commerce sites may take 8-12 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

What makes your websites different from others?

Our websites are built with a focus on performance, user experience, and conversion optimization. We use advanced technologies and follow best practices for SEO, resulting in faster loading times and higher search rankings.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance services?

Yes, we offer flexible maintenance packages that include regular updates, security monitoring, backup management, and technical support. Our team is available 24/7 for emergency assistance.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing starts at $2,999 for basic business websites and varies based on your specific needs and requirements. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your project scope during the initial consultation.
